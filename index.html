About ICETT '26

The International Conference on Emerging Trends and Transformations (ICETT '26) is a premier global event dedicated to exploring cutting-edge innovations and transformative ideas that are redefining the future of science and technology.

ICETT '26 brings together leading researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and thought leaders to share insights, present research breakthroughs, and discuss impactful trends shaping tomorrow's world. Organized by the School of Computer Science and Engineering (SCOPE), VIT-AP University, Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh.

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering was established in 2017 and became the School of Computer Science and Engineering (SCOPE) in January 2019. The school provides the necessary infrastructure, hardware, and software to support faculty and student research through experiential learning and new pedagogical approaches.
